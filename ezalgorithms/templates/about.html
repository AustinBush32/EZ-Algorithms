Start Learning Algorithms

Start learning algorithms today in the simplest and easiest way possible. Whether you are a seasoned programmer or a beginner, EZ Algorithms is a valuable resource to teach you a new algorithm or simply refresh your memory. Begin by clicking on “Lessons” and select the category of algorithms you are looking for to view our catalog.

Mission

Our mission is to make learning algorithms easy. We want to provide only the information necessary to learn and use an algorithm. Gone are the days of sifting through long and confusing text books or web pages to grasp the algorithm concepts. EZ Algorithms will contain lessons on most algorithms in topic such as sorting, dynamic programming, greedy algorithms and graph algorithms.

Approach

Each lesson will contain the bare minimum necessary for you to learn and use an algorithm in addition to a programming problem that uses the skills in that lesson to re-enforce you're learning. I encourage everyone to attempt each programming problem on their own, but a solution will be available for anyone who wants to view it.

About Me

This website was created and is maintained by one person. I am currently a Software Engineering student and one of my favorite hobbies is competitive programming. I enjoy learning about and using algorithms to solve problems, so what better way to enhance what I enjoy doing than to share my knowledge with everyone! I hope to inspire anyone interested in computer science to continue to learn and use their skills by demonstrating how easy easy it is to use algorithms.
